preserve_aspect_ratio(align, meet_or_slice='meet')

Specifies how the image within a viewport should be scaled.

align
a combination of ‘xMin’, ‘xMid’, or ‘xMax’, followed by ‘YMin’, ‘YMid’, or ‘YMax’
meet_or_slice
one of ‘meet’ or ‘slice’
