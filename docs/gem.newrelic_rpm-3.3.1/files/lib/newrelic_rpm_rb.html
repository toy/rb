New Relic Initialization

When installed as a gem, you can activate the New Relic agent one of the following ways:

For Rails, add: 

   config.gem 'newrelic_rpm'

to your initialization sequence.

For merb, do 

   dependency 'newrelic_rpm'

in the Merb config/init.rb

For Sinatra, do 

   require 'newrelic_rpm'

after requiring ‘sinatra’.

For other frameworks, or to manage the agent manually, invoke NewRelic::Agent#manual_start directly.
