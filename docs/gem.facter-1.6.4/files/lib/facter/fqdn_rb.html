Fact: fqdn

Purpose: Returns the fully qualified domain name of the host.

Resolution: Simply joins the hostname fact with the domain name fact.

Caveats: No attempt is made to check that the two facts are accurate or that the two facts go together. At no point is there any DNS resolution made either.
