A Jeweler helps you craft the perfect Rubygem. Give him a gemspec, and he takes care of the rest.

See Jeweler::Tasks for examples of how to get started. Additionally, resources are available on the wiki:

  • wiki.github.com/technicalpickles/jeweler/create-a-new-project
  • wiki.github.com/technicalpickles/jeweler/configure-an-existing-project
